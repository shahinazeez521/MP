Natural History Museum Abu Dhabi

Role :

BIM Lead Architect

Project Details

The Natural History Museum Abu Dhabi, opening in 2025 on Saadiyat Island, will be the region’s largest. Designed by Mecanoo, the 35,000 m² museum will trace 13.8 billion years of history, showcasing “Stan” the T. rex, the Murchison meteorite, and world-class research facilities.
